WHY SUPPORT US?

Hundreds of eager students quit VEX each year simply because they can’t find a clear path forward. Not all teams are lucky to have invested coaches or volunteers for the program. Some schools don’t recognize VEX the way they do other sports or programs. That leaves students reinventing the wheel, stuck in a loop of trial and error, and eventually just copying the best design they can find on YouTube. We’re not sending robots to schools. We’re breaking down barriers by sharing everything you need to start and sustain a VEX team.

HOW TO SUPPORT US

Monetary Support: Direct financial support will help support our Mentors and VEX U team with raw parts procurement, registration fees, competitions, and travel. Additionally, this allows us to provide more resources to the community through the production of educational resources and events.

Products & Service: By donating products or services, you give students direct exposure to your brand and let them experience your offerings without worrying about cost. We can also showcase your products through reviews and recommend them to our community of teams and educators.

Please contact us at socalalliancenonprofit@gmail.com for any clarifications and support tier benefits.
