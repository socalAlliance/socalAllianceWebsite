WHAT IS VEX ROBOTICS?

VEX V5 Robotics lets students tackle open-ended engineering challenges by designing, building, and programming robots in a competitive, team-based setting. It emphasizes creative problem-solving, iterative design, and collaboration under real constraints. Through this, students gain a practical understanding of how engineers approach complex problems from concept to execution.
