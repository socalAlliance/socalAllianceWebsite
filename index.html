SOCAL ALLIANCE

SoCal Alliance was founded in 2023 after a simple act of generosity—one team lending spare parts to another at a critical moment—which blossomed into a community grounded in mutual respect and shared innovation.

Today, over 180 members strong, we unite passionate VEX Robotics teams for high-level performance, collective learning, and groundbreaking ideas, all while supporting each other every step of the way.

I’m proud to serve as part of the SCA Nonprofit Initiative and look forward to empowering teams within and beyond our network.

Winston Lin • SoCal Alliance Founder
